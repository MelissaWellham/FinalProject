About

Melissa Wellham is a freelance writer, social media manager and digital marketer.

Personal

I have nerd-levels of love for everything I do - including my work - with experience in journalism, content creation, digital marketing, social media management, and web content management.

Professional

I previously worked as a writer and website coordinator at Mamamia.com.au from 2012 – 2014, and worked as the film editor and writer for BMA Magazine from 2011 – 2015. My work has recently been published in Birdee Mag, Feminartsy, Your Zen Life, 1 Million Women and Oxfam’s 3 Things blog, among others.
